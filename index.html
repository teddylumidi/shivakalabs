FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request a service?+
Simply click any "Get Help on WhatsApp" button, or message us directly at 0716 358 703. Tell us what you need and we'll guide you through the entire process professionally.
How do I pay for services?+
Payments are made via M-Pesa or bank transfer. Fees are discussed upfront before work begins. Government fees (e.g. passport, DCI) are paid directly on eCitizen — we only charge for our professional assistance.
How long does processing take?+
Most requests are handled same day or within 24 hours. Government processing times vary — passports take 10–21 days, while KRA PIN is instant. We keep you updated throughout.
Do you help with eCitizen account issues?+
Yes. We assist with account creation, password reset, login recovery, payment guidance, form filling, document uploads and appointment booking — everything eCitizen-related.
Do you also build websites?+
Absolutely. We design and develop professional websites for businesses, NGOs, schools, and individuals — from simple landing pages to full e-commerce stores.
Can you help both individuals and businesses?+
Yes — we serve individuals (documents, CVs, applications), small businesses, NGOs, schools and large organisations. Whatever your need, we're here to help.
