## TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

### All PNG Files Should Have:
- ✅ **Format:** PNG (24-bit or 32-bit with alpha)
- ✅ **Transparency:** Transparent backgrounds where needed
- ✅ **Compression:** Optimized (use TinyPNG or similar)
- ✅ **Color Space:** sRGB (web-standard)
- ✅ **DPI:** 72 DPI (web standard)
- ✅ **Interlacing:** None (progressive PNG not needed for web)

### Responsive Image Sizing:
- **Large images (hero, banners):** 1920px wide max
- **Medium images (section features):** 400-600px wide  
- **Small images (card assets):** 200-300px wide
- **Tiny images (icons):** 64px-128px wide

### Optimization:
- Target **<200KB per image** for performance
- Use TinyPNG (tinypng.com) for compression
- Consider WebP format for future optimization
- Test image load times
