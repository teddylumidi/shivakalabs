Good Conduct Certificate Service Overview

We provide comprehensive assistance with obtaining Good Conduct Certificates from the Kenyan government. Whether you need it for employment, education, travel, or any other purpose, we handle the entire process on your behalf. Simply provide your details, and we take care of the rest using the official eCitizen portal.

What We Offer:

  • Complete Document Preparation: We verify all your details and ensure everything is correct before submission
  • eCitizen Portal Management: We handle all navigation, form filling, and submission on the government portal
  • Real-Time Updates: Regular WhatsApp updates on your application status
  • Turnaround Time: Typical processing time is 3-5 business days from submission
  • Document Delivery: Digital copy sent via email upon approval (physical copy available separately)

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get the certificate?

Typically 3-5 business days from the time we submit your application on eCitizen. We'll keep you updated via WhatsApp throughout the process.

Why do you need my ID details?

We need your ID or Passport number to access the eCitizen portal on your behalf. This is a standard requirement for government services. Your information is treated with utmost confidentiality.

Is this service legal?

Yes, absolutely. We're a legitimate service provider authorized to assist citizens with government services. We work directly with the eCitizen platform on your behalf.

What if my application is rejected?

Rejections are rare, but if it happens, we'll analyze the reason and resubmit with corrected information at no additional charge. We'll work with you until you get your certificate.

Can you get a hardcopy instead of digital?

Yes, you can collect your hardcopy from the respective government office. The digital copy we provide can be printed as needed for most purposes.
