KRA PIN Service Overview

A KRA PIN (Kenya Revenue Authority Personal Identification Number) is essential for anyone doing business or working as a self-employed professional in Kenya. We make the registration process simple and stress-free. Our team handles all the paperwork and eCitizen portal submissions, ensuring you get your PIN quickly and correctly.

Why You Need a KRA PIN:

  • Required for business registration and operation in Kenya
  • Necessary to open a business bank account
  • Required for government tenders and contracts
  • Needed for self-employed professionals and freelancers
  • Required when importing or exporting goods
  • Essential for e-commerce and online business operations

What We Offer:

  • Document Preparation: We ensure all required documents are prepared and verified
  • eCitizen Management: We complete your registration on the government portal
  • Fast Processing: Usually get your PIN within 1-3 business days
  • Email Delivery: PIN sent directly to your email upon approval

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does KRA PIN registration take?

Usually 1-3 business days. You'll receive your PIN via email. We provide WhatsApp updates throughout the process.

Do I need a business to get a KRA PIN?

No. Any individual 18+ years old can register for a KRA PIN, whether self-employed, freelance, or starting a business. We assist with all types of registrations.

Is the KRA PIN registration free?

Yes, the government registration is completely free. We only charge KES 300 for our professional assistance in handling the application process for you.

Can I get a KRA PIN for an existing business?

Yes absolutely. If your business doesn't have a KRA PIN yet, we can help you register one. We also assist with PIN updates and modifications.

What if I lose my KRA PIN?

We can help you retrieve your PIN details from KRA records. Contact us with your ID number and we'll recover it for you within 24 hours.
