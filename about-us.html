Who We Are

Professional Digital Solutions Since 2015

ShivakaLabs is a professional digital services provider based in Kenya. We are dedicated to helping individuals and businesses navigate the digital landscape with confidence and ease. Our core expertise spans government eCitizen portal assistance, custom website development, stunning graphic design, and strategic digital marketing solutions.

With extensive experience in online services, we transform complex government applications, website projects, and digital marketing campaigns into simple, fast, and stress-free solutions — all accessible via WhatsApp. We pride ourselves on delivering quality, reliability, and outstanding customer service.
