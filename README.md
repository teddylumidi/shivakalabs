# ShivakaLabs - Professional Digital Services Company

Welcome to the **ShivakaLabs** website repository! We are a full-service digital agency offering web development, mobile app development, graphic design, digital marketing, and government eCitizen services.

## 🌟 About ShivakaLabs

**Mission:** To empower businesses and individuals through innovative digital solutions that drive growth, efficiency, and success in the modern digital economy.

**Vision:** To be the most trusted and innovative digital agency in East Africa, known for excellence, integrity, and transformative solutions.

## 📋 Services Offered

- **Web Development** - Custom websites and web applications
- **Mobile App Development** - Native and cross-platform mobile apps
- **Graphic Design** - Branding, logos, and visual design
- **Digital Marketing** - SEO, social media, and digital advertising
- **eCitizen Services** - Government portal registration and assistance
- **Business Consulting** - Digital transformation and strategy
